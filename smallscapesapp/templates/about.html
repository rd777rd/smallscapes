Our Story & Philosophy

Crafting Long-Lasting Masonry Foundations

SmallScapes is a highly committed landscaping and specialized hardscape construction company based in Indiana. Founded on a simple mission—to build structural beauty and restore failing yards—we have grown into a premier provider of residential stone-masonry services.

Unlike general lawn mowers, our true expertise lies in the physical geometry of hardscaping: brick paver laying. We pride ourselves on the meticulous, non-visible engineering that makes hardscapes permanent. Every patio and garden walkway we build is configured with a robust gravel subgrade, dense bedding sand, concrete edge restrains, and genuine joint locking polymer sand.

This ensures that your pavers stay absolutely flat, perfectly interlocked, and free of weeds, defying Indiana's aggressive frost, heavy clay soils, and seasonal humidity cycle after cycle.

Standard Process

How SmallScapes Crafts Your Yard

01

I

Survey & Slope Check

We inspect physical properties, assess drainage vectors, plot elevation lines, and specify precise layouts.

02

II

Subbase Prep

Excavation down to pristine levels, laying heavy geotextile fabric, followed by gravel subbase compaction.

03

III

Paver Placement

Fine sand screeding, placement of brick pavers in your desired pattern, and embedding side constraints.

04

IV

Polymer Locking

Laying high-performance binding joint sand, plate vibrating, and locking everything with specialized sealants.
